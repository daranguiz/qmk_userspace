Option 1 Refinements - Bigger Fonts & Tighter Trigger Spacing

Testing different ways to bring the trigger closer to the arrow with larger fonts.

Variation 1: Right-Aligned Trigger

Trigger right-aligned (70px width), 18px trigger, 20px output. Brings trigger close to arrow.

Words & Symbols
